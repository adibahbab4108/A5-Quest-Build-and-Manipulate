Important FAQ

1. What are the primary causes supported by your donations?

We focus on several key areas, including:

  • Relief for flood-affected areas such as Feni, Sylhet, Noakhali, and Cox's Bazar.
  • Medical aid and rehabilitation for those injured in the quota movement.
  • Poverty alleviation through sustainable development programs.
  • Educational support and empowerment for underprivileged children and communities.
2. How is my donation used in flood-affected areas?

Your donation helps provide emergency relief, including food, clean water, shelter, and medical assistance, to families affected by flooding in Feni, Sylhet, Noakhali, and Cox's Bazar. Long-term efforts also focus on rebuilding homes, schools, and infrastructure.

3. How can I ensure that my donation reaches the people in need?

We partner with local NGOs and volunteers to ensure that aid is distributed directly to those in need. Regular reports and updates are shared with donors about how and where funds are utilized.

4. How do you help individuals injured during the quota movement?

We provide medical care, rehabilitation, and psychological support for people injured during the quota movement protests. We also support families in managing medical expenses and ensure long-term recovery assistance.

5. Can I choose which cause my donation supports?

Yes, when making a donation, you can specify whether you want to support flood relief, medical aid for the quota movement, poverty alleviation, or educational programs for underprivileged communities.

6. What are the long-term strategies for poverty alleviation?

Our poverty alleviation programs focus on skill development, microfinance initiatives, and small business support to help families become self-sufficient. We also prioritize access to education, healthcare, and clean water.

7. How can I help underprivileged children in Bangladesh?

You can contribute to our education and nutrition programs that provide school supplies, meals, and scholarships to underprivileged children. We also run mentorship programs to encourage them to pursue higher education and vocational training.

8. Can I volunteer to help in these efforts?

Yes, we welcome volunteers both locally and internationally. You can participate in on-ground relief work in flood-affected areas, mentor underprivileged children, or help us raise awareness through online campaigns.

9. How do you ensure transparency in the use of donations?

We maintain full transparency by publishing detailed financial reports, including how donations are spent. We also provide regular updates on our projects and success stories to keep donors informed.

10. Are there tax benefits for donating to this cause?

Yes, donations made to registered charitable organizations may be eligible for tax deductions. Please consult with your local tax advisor to determine eligibility based on your country's laws.
